# Debt Freedom Planner Sora Prompt Package

## Master prompt

Create a 35-second polished commercial for **Debt Freedom Planner**, a debt payoff planning product with a calm but proudly American visual identity. The product should feel trustworthy, practical, and hopeful, not cartoonish. Use the supplied shield logo as the hero brand mark and build the look around deep navy, clean white, bright liberty blue, and disciplined red accents.

### Creative direction

- Tone: patriotic, reassuring, determined, family-safe, practical
- Audience: adults 35-65+ who want a simple road out of debt
- Visual language: sunlight across porches, pickup tailgates, kitchen tables, printed plans, laptop dashboard closeups, subtle flag-inspired light bands, clear typography overlays, strong-but-not-cheesy Americana
- Product promise: "See your fastest path to debt freedom."
- Avoid: political slogans, campaign imagery, military fetish imagery, aggressive nationalism, gimmicky eagles everywhere, fintech neon, crypto aesthetics

### Product shots to include

- Shield logo opening reveal with metallic light sweep
- Laptop and tablet showing the Debt Freedom Planner dashboard
- A hand entering debts and selecting snowball vs avalanche
- A printed payoff plan on a kitchen table beside a coffee mug and bills
- A family or couple looking relieved after seeing the debt-free date
- A closing lockup with the logo and headline

### Text overlays

- "One clear road out of debt"
- "Know your debt-free date"
- "Compare payoff strategies in minutes"
- "Print a plan your family can follow"
- "Debt Freedom Planner"

### Voiceover

"You do not need another spreadsheet. You need a clear road. Debt Freedom Planner shows your debt-free date, compares your payoff options, and gives you a plan you can actually follow. One household. One strategy. One clear path forward. Debt Freedom Planner. See your fastest path to debt freedom."

### Technical goals

- 16:9
- crisp commercial lighting
- premium product-ad pacing
- 4k feel
- subtle motion graphics over UI screens
- legible UI inserts
- end card holds for 3 seconds
